## Value Proposition

- **Problem:** Camera rolls become too large and tedious to clean with traditional multi-select screens, causing storage pressure and making useful memories harder to find.
- **Target Audience:** People with cluttered photo libraries who want a quick, low-effort, deliberate way to decide what stays and what goes.
- **Unique Differentiator:** Duplicate cleanup plus a focused, game-like swipe flow—right to keep, left to yeet—with month and media filters and review-before-delete reassurance.
- **Elevator Pitch:** KeepYeet turns camera-roll cleanup into a quick swipe habit, helping people keep the memories that matter and remove the clutter that does not.
