## Data Collection and Processing

KeepYeet is an iOS mobile application designed to help users manage, organise, and clean their photo galleries **locally on their devices**. We do not upload, store, or process your photos or videos on our own servers. Your photo library, sorting decisions, and deletion actions stay on-device.

After you tap `Continue` on the welcome screen, KeepYeet may initialize connected third-party services used for analytics, advertising, and purchase support. Those services may collect or receive limited data such as:

- Device identifiers, including advertising identifiers and SDK-generated identifiers;
- User or account-linked identifiers used by our partners for analytics and purchase attribution;
- IP address;
- Approximate or coarse location derived by partners from device or network signals;
- Operating system, device type, app version, and build information;
- App performance data, crash logs, and other diagnostic data;
- Product interaction data, including screens viewed, taps, session activity, and ad interaction data;
- Purchase history, entitlement status, and transaction-related metadata.

KeepYeet uses this data for the following purposes:

- Analytics and performance monitoring;
- In-app advertising and ad measurement;
- Purchase processing, entitlement management, and restore flows;
- Fraud prevention, abuse prevention, and app support.

KeepYeet does **not** use session replay, and it does **not** upload or store your photos or videos outside your device.

## Data Storage and Protection

KeepYeet does not operate its own backend for user photos or gallery data. Your media remains on your device. Third-party services described in this policy may process limited technical, advertising, analytics, and purchase data on their own infrastructure.

PostHog is configured to use an **EU host**. Other third-party providers, including Google, RevenueCat, and Apple, may process data in the EU, the US, and other regions in accordance with their own privacy policies and transfer mechanisms.

### Data Protection Measures

- **Encryption:** Our third-party partners use encryption and other safeguards to protect data in transit and at rest.
- **Access control:** Access to data processed by those partners is limited according to their internal access policies and business needs.

## Tracking and Advertising

KeepYeet uses **Google AdMob** to display in-app advertisements. After you tap `Continue` on the welcome screen, the app may request permission under Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. If you allow tracking, AdMob may use applicable identifiers to help deliver and measure personalised advertising. If you deny tracking, KeepYeet will request non-personalized ads where applicable.

KeepYeet also uses analytics to understand product usage and app performance. Those analytics are not described as anonymous for App Store disclosure purposes because linked identifiers may be used by our analytics and purchase partners for measurement and attribution.
